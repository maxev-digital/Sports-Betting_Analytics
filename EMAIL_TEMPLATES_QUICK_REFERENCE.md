## 💡 PRO TIPS

### Email Best Practices:
1. **Send Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM EST** (best response times)
2. **Personalize subject line** if sending manually
3. **Use professional email signature**
4. **BCC yourself** to track what you sent
5. **Follow up after 7 days** if no response

### Personalization Tips:
- Replace `[COMPANY NAME]` with actual name
- Add specific details if you know them (e.g., "I noticed you recently expanded to Ohio")
- Reference their website if you saw something specific
- Mention any mutual connections

### Response Management:
- **Create folder:** "Affiliate Applications - Responses"
- **Track in spreadsheet:** Date sent, date responded, status, notes
- **Respond within 24 hours** to all replies
- **Save all pricing quotes** for comparison
