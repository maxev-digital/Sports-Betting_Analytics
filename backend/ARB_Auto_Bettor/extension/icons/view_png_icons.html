New Extension Icon: White Circle with Blue Lightning Bolt

16x16 (Toolbar)

32x32 (Small)

48x48 (Medium)

128x128 (Large)

To Apply the New Icon:

1. Go to chrome://extensions/

2. Find "ARB Auto Bettor"

3. Click Remove

4. Click Load unpacked

5. Select: C:\Users\nashr\backend\ARB_Auto_Bettor\extension

The white circle with blue lightning will now appear in your toolbar!
