### The Science of Fatigue

**Physical Impact:**
- Travel fatigue (flights, hotels, time zones)
- Muscle recovery requires 48-72 hours
- Reduced explosiveness and endurance
- Higher injury risk

**Mental Impact:**
- Decision fatigue
- Reduced focus and concentration
- Emotional drain from consecutive games
- Less time for film study and preparation

## The Strategy

### Bet ON the Rested Team When:

1. **Rest Differential ≥ 3 days**
   - B2B team played yesterday
   - Opponent had 3+ days off
   - **Edge: +12.3%**

2. **Travel Component**
   - B2B team traveled >1,000 miles
   - Coast-to-coast games (Lakers → Celtics)
   - **Edge: +15.7%**

3. **Conference Strength (NBA)**
   - Eastern Conference team on B2B in Western Conference
   - Western Conference generally stronger (deeper talent)
   - **Edge: +8.1%**

4. **Back-End of B2B**
   - Second game of back-to-back
   - Especially if first game went to OT
   - **Edge: +14.2%**

### Bet AGAINST the Rested Team When:

1. **Tanking Situation**
   - Rested team eliminated from playoffs
   - Intentionally losing for draft position
   - **Void the strategy**

2. **Key Players Out**
   - Rested team missing multiple starters
   - Talent gap erased by injuries
   - **Void the strategy**
