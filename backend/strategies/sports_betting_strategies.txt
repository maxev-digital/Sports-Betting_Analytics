Sports Betting Strategies Compilation (With Risk Assessments and Historical Performance)

This file compiles all live betting strategies for major pro and college sports, including NHL. It includes strategy details such as triggers, rationale, opportunities, and model integrations. Risk assessments evaluate volatility, frequency, edge reliability, bankroll impact, and overall risk level (Low: stable; Medium: balanced; High: volatile). Historical performance data is included from 2007-2025 seasons where available, based on sourced analyses (e.g., ROI, win rates). General trends are noted where specific data is limited.

Use this for independent development with tools like Claude Code in your Python/MongoDB/Google Sheets project.

NHL Strategies
NHL live betting focuses on low-scoring games with momentum, power plays, and goaltending.

1. Momentum Shift Betting (Mid-Game Dominance)
   Triggers: Live Corsi >60% over last 5 min; xG differential +0.5; model shows >55% next-goal prob vs. book odds.
   Rationale: Games flip on momentum; books slow to adjust.
   Opportunities: Next goal (team A -120), period total over 1.5 (+150).
   Model Integration: Update xG with live shots; compare to historical.
   Risk Assessment: Medium - Moderate volatility from quick shifts; frequent (2-4 per game); reliable edge if xG accurate; stake 1-3% bankroll; medium due to data-driven triggers.
   Historical Performance: Favorites win 58.6% vs. expected 58.2% (2007-2022); multivariate models 8.5% ROI on high-confidence plays (2016-2021, 249 games).

2. Power Play Exploitation (Special Teams Edges)
   Triggers: Power play starts; PP xG >0.3 per min; GSAx of PK goalie < -0.1.
   Rationale: Power plays convert ~20-25%; target mismatches.
   Opportunities: Goal in next 2 min (+200), player prop over 0.5 points.
   Model Integration: Poisson for PP goals; Bayesian with fantasy ratings.
   Risk Assessment: High - High volatility (short/unpredictable); infrequent (1-3 per game); edge reliable but variance high; stake 1-2% bankroll; high risk in playoffs.
   Historical Performance: PP conversion 21.18% (highest since 1985-86); medium edge in mismatches ~20-25% success.

3. Goalie Performance Swings (Hot/Cold Streaks)
   Triggers: Save% <0.850 over last 10 shots; GSAx < -0.2.
   Rationale: Goalies dictate outcomes; pulling creates edges.
   Opportunities: Over remaining goals (+110), team to win if hot.
   Model Integration: Real-time GSAx in XGBoost; historical baselines.
   Risk Assessment: Medium - Volatility from streaks; frequent in high-shot games; GSAx reliable; stake 2-4% bankroll; medium as data stabilizes.
   Historical Performance: Hot streaks boost win prob 20-30%; no specific ROI.

4. Late-Game Scenarios (Empty Net and Lead Protection)
   Triggers: Empty net; possession <40% for trailing team.
   Rationale: ~50% empty-net scores; high edge on chaos.
   Opportunities: Empty-net goal yes (-150), total under.
   Model Integration: Survival analysis; Monte Carlo sims.
   Risk Assessment: High - Extreme volatility in final minutes; infrequent (end-game); edge high but chaotic; stake 1-2% bankroll; high due to desperation.
   Historical Performance: Empty-net goals ~44%; win rates ~50% for yes bets.

5. Period-Specific Betting (Pace Adjustments)
   Triggers: 1st period shots >15; Poisson λ >1.2.
   Rationale: Periods vary; 1st high-scoring.
   Opportunities: Period winner (+200), over 1.5 in high-pace.
   Model Integration: Markov chains; incorporate travel.
   Risk Assessment: Low - Low volatility per period; frequent (3 periods/game); reliable pacing; stake 2-5% bankroll; low as segmented.
   Historical Performance: 1st period under hits 64-67% (2022-23, ROI 10-27%).

6. Player Prop Explosions (Individual Hot Streaks)
   Triggers: Shots >3 in 1st; RAPM >+0.5.
   Rationale: Stars explode; books lag.
   Opportunities: Over shots 4.5 (+110).
   Model Integration: Agent-based with EA attributes.
   Risk Assessment: High - High volatility from individual variance; infrequent; edge spotty; stake 1-3% bankroll; high due to unpredictability.
   Historical Performance: Prop win rates ~50-55%; no ROI cited.

7. Arbitrage and Line Shopping (Multi-Book Edges)
   Triggers: Model prob > book by 5%.
   Rationale: Lines vary; exploit delays.
   Opportunities: Middling totals, prop arbs.
   Model Integration: Ensemble compares to feeds.
   Risk Assessment: Low - Low volatility (locks profit); variable frequency; highly reliable; stake 3-5% bankroll; low if multi-books.
   Historical Performance: Arbitrage rare; 2.6-8.5% ROI on high-confidence plays (2016-2021).

8. Fatigue and Schedule Spots (Back-to-Back or Travel)
   Triggers: Back-to-back; speed < average.
   Rationale: Schedule grinds; fresher edges.
   Opportunities: Underdog moneylines (+200), under totals.
   Model Integration: Glicko-2; historical.
   Risk Assessment: Medium - Moderate volatility; infrequent (schedule-dependent); reliable historical; stake 2-4% bankroll; medium due to external factors.
   Historical Performance: Back-to-backs drop 0.108 points/game; win rates -4-5%.

   Risk Assessment: High - High volatility; frequent (2-5 per game); edge variable in college; stake 1-3% bankroll; high due to randomness.

   Risk Assessment: Low - Low volatility; once per game; high reliability; stake 3-5% bankroll; low as predictable.

4. Weather/Fatigue Plays (Late-Game)

General Platform Integration
- Edge Calculation: (Model prob - book prob) / book prob; Kelly stake = (edge * odds - 1) / (odds - 1) * bankroll fraction.
- Alerts: Audible via Chrome extension; pre-fill bets, auto-navigate to books.
- Models: Use ensembles (e.g., 40% regression, 30% ML); Bayesian for updates.
- Thresholds: Alert if edge >5%; 3-10 alerts per event.
- Risk Management: Incorporate assessments in alerts (e.g., "High Risk: Stake <2%"); user-set limits in Sheets.

Workflows
- Daily: Ingest odds/stats (8 AM); compute deltas; update Mongo; run models; sync dashboard.
- Weekly: Merge historical; retrain ensembles; simulate slates.
- Live (Per-Event): Poll APIs 10-60s (sport-dependent); ETL; scan strategies; alert; log.
  - NFL/NBA/MLB/MLS: 10s polls.
  - Tennis/UFC/Golf: 30s.
  - NASCAR/Track: 60s.
- College: Similar, filter major conferences.

Data Sources
- Odds: The Odds API (primary); OddsMatrix for faster live.
- Stats: SportsDataIO, Sportradar (NHL official), ESPN scraping (prototype only).
- Advanced: MoneyPuck (NHL xG), Evolving-Hockey (GAR/RAPM), Rotoworld (injuries).
- Player Ratings: Dobber Hockey, EA Sports, Fantasy platforms (ADP).
- APIs: NHL API, NBA API, MLB Statcast; costs from $50/mo (SportsDataIO) to enterprise (Sportradar).
