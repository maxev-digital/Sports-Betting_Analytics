# Professional Design Update Applied

## Changes Made Across All Pages:

### Border Styling:
- Changed from `border` to `border-2` for more prominent borders
- Changed from `border-slate-700` to maintain consistency
- Hover states changed to `border-blue-600` for interactivity

### Border Radius:
- Changed from `rounded-lg` to `rounded` for sharper corners
- Changed from `rounded-xl` to `rounded-lg` where applicable
- More professional, less rounded appearance

### Color Palette - Focused on Red, Blue, White, Black, Green:
- **Green**: Success states, high confidence, profit, OVER bets (green-600/700)
- **Red**: Warnings, UNDER bets, losses (red-600/700)
- **Blue**: Medium confidence, spreads, information (blue-600/700)
- **White**: Primary text, high contrast
- **Black/Slate**: Backgrounds and borders (slate-800/900, slate-700)

### Emojis Replaced with Logos:
- Sport league logos from ESPN CDN
- Professional branded appearance
- Consistent sizing (w-4 h-4 for inline, w-5 h-5 for buttons)

### Typography:
- Added `font-bold` to headers
- Added `tracking-wide` for uppercase labels
- Increased contrast with white text
- Consistent font weights throughout

### Button Styling:
- More solid backgrounds
- Thicker borders (border-2)
- Less transparency
- Sharper corners

### Card Styling:
- Solid backgrounds instead of transparent overlays
- `bg-slate-800` and `bg-slate-900` for depth
- Border-2 for all cards
- Consistent padding
