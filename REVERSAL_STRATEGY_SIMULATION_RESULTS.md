## 💰 KELLY CRITERION PARAMETERS

### Full Kelly Calculation:
```
Formula: f = (bp - q) / b
where:
  b = net odds (0.91 for -110)
  p = win probability (0.556)
  q = loss probability (0.444)

Full Kelly = (0.91 * 0.556 - 0.444) / 0.91 = 6.5%
```

### 1/4 Kelly Applied:
```
1/4 Kelly = 6.5% * 0.25 = 1.62% per bet
Safety cap at 5% of bankroll
```

### Bet Sizing Examples:

| Bankroll | 1/4 Kelly Bet | Max Bet (5% cap) |
|----------|--------------|------------------|
| $20,000 | $324 | $1,000 |
| $50,000 | $810 | $2,500 |
